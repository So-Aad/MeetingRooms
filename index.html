Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a meeting room?

Simply click the "Book Now" button, select your preferred room type, date, and time, and complete the booking process. You'll receive an instant confirmation email.

What's your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before your booking. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a fee.

Do you offer discounts for regular bookings?

Yes! We offer special rates for monthly bookings and corporate accounts. Contact our sales team for more information.

What amenities are included?

All rooms include high-speed Wi-Fi, basic AV equipment, and booking.
